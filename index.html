Trackrrr is a period tracker with privacy and inclusivity in mind.

Features

Keep your data on your phone

The data entered in the app is stored locally on your device, not on any servers.

See your data

See insights about your data, including visualizations

Available in different languages

Trackrrr is available in English, French, Spanish, Swedish, and Arabic, with more languages on the way.

Dark and Light modes

Use the app in your preferred appearance mode.

Customizable notifications

Get notified for your upcoming period so that you can prepare accordingly. Configure push notifications to receive only the ones you care about. Customize text for more discrete (or fun!) notifications.

Import and Export your data

Import or export your period tracking data as CSV.
