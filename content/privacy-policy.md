## Android Permissions

The **Trackrrr** app does not require any permissions on Android.


## User Data

On Android, all period data is stored on the device in SQLite in the `./databases` folder and the **Trackrrr** app does not send this data to any servers.

On web, no data is collected.

This Log Data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser version, pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics.
